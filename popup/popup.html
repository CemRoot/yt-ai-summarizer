Follow these steps once. You do not need to be technical—just click where it says.

  1. 1
    Open YouTube in Chrome and play any video that has captions (CC).
  2. 2
    Click the puzzle icon in Chrome’s toolbar (top right). Find YouTube AI Summarizer and pin it if you like. Then click the extension icon to open this window.
  3. 3
    Go to the Setup tab. Choose Groq or Ollama Cloud. Paste your API key in the box. Use the blue “Get … key” link if you do not have one yet—it opens the sign-up page in a new tab.
  4. 4
    Open the Options tab. Pick your output language (or leave Auto). For AI podcast voices, add a Gemini key in the podcast section—only if you use that feature.
  5. 5
    Press Save Settings at the bottom. Wait until it says “Saved”.
  6. 6
    On the video page, click the red floating button (or open the side panel). Choose Summary, Key Points, or Chat. The text follows your language and caption choices on YouTube.
Tabs in short:
  • Setup — AI provider and API keys.
  • Options — language, podcast key, defaults and caching.
  • Help — this guide.

Problems? Try Clear Cache, reload the YouTube tab, and make sure captions match the language you want. Links at the bottom go to provider docs and privacy.
