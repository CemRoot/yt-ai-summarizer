1 Data We Access

  • YouTube transcripts — extracted only when you click "Summarize".
  • Your API keys (Groq, Ollama, Gemini TTS) — stored locally in your browser for authentication.
  • Extension preferences — provider, model, language, cache toggles.

2 How Data Is Used

  • Transcript text is sent to your chosen AI provider (Groq or Ollama Cloud) solely for summarization.
  • Podcast script is sent to Google Gemini TTS solely for audio generation.
  • API keys are used only for authenticating requests to the provider you selected.
  • No personal data (name, email, IP, browsing history) is ever collected.

3 Storage & Retention

Everything is stored locally in your browser via Chrome's extension storage API.

Summaries: optional cache (LRU, max 20) Transcripts: off by default API keys: obfuscated (XOR + Base64)

Use the "Clear Cache" button in settings or remove the extension to wipe all data instantly.

4 Third-Party Services

  • Groq API (groq.com) — summarization. See Groq Privacy Policy.
  • Ollama Cloud (ollama.com) — summarization. See Ollama Terms.
  • Google Gemini TTS (ai.google.dev) — podcast audio only. See Google AI Terms.

We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any data collection services.

5 Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share data with third parties beyond your chosen AI provider
  • Use your data for advertising or profiling
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect telemetry or usage analytics

6 Permissions

7 Your Rights

  • Clear cached summaries and transcripts from the extension panel at any time.
  • Revoke your Groq key at console.groq.com/keys.
  • Revoke your Ollama key at ollama.com/settings/keys.
  • Revoke your Gemini key at aistudio.google.com/apikey.
  • Remove the extension to delete all stored data permanently.

8 Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
