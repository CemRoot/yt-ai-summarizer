Privacy Policy

YouTube AI Summarizer — how we handle data. No telemetry, no publisher servers; your keys and choices stay on your device.

Last updated March 31, 2026 Extension version v1.8.1

1 Data we access

  • YouTube transcripts — Captions for the video on the tab you are viewing are read only when you start a summary, chat, or podcast flow. We do not scrape unrelated pages.
  • Your API keys (Groq, Ollama Cloud, Google Gemini for TTS) — Stored locally for authentication to those services only.
  • Extension preferences — Provider, model, language, cache toggles, and defaults. All stored locally.

2 How data is used

  • Summaries — Transcript text is sent to your chosen AI provider (Groq or Ollama Cloud) to generate summary, key points, and detailed analysis. Responses return to your browser only.
  • Chat — When you use the Chat tab, your messages, a slice of transcript context, and recent conversation history are sent to the same provider to generate replies. Nothing is logged by us; we do not operate a backend.
  • Podcast — A generated script may be sent to Google Gemini TTS to produce audio, which is played in the browser and may be cached locally per your settings.
  • API keys — Used only to authenticate to the provider you selected. They are not transmitted to the extension author or unrelated third parties.
  • We do not collect your name, email, account identifiers, full browsing history, or IP address for our own purposes.

3 Storage & retention

Settings and optional caches are stored with Chrome’s chrome.storage.local API. The extension does not send that data to publisher-controlled servers.

Summaries: optional LRU cache (max 20 videos) Transcripts: optional; off by default API keys: obfuscated locally (not plaintext) Chat: in-memory for the current page session

Clear data anytime via the panel trash control or Clear Cache in extension settings. Uninstalling the extension removes extension storage.

4 Third-party services

Depending on your settings, requests may go to:

  • Groq — groq.com · Privacy policy
  • Ollama Cloud — ollama.com · Terms
  • Google Gemini (TTS) — ai.google.dev · AI terms

We do not embed analytics, ads, or cross-site trackers in the extension.

5 Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share it for advertising or profiling. Data leaves your browser only when you trigger a feature that calls a third-party API you configured (see above).

7 Your rights

  • Delete cached content and reset options from the extension UI.
  • Revoke Groq keys at console.groq.com/keys
  • Revoke Ollama keys at ollama.com/settings/keys
  • Revoke Gemini keys at aistudio.google.com/apikey
  • Remove the extension to delete its stored data from your browser.

8 Children’s privacy

The extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has disclosed data in a way that concerns you, contact us (below).

9 Compliance & safety

Open-source codebase (MIT). Architecture uses modular ES classes and a single storage helper for settings — you can review behavior on GitHub. Runtime diagnostics use console.error only for serious failures (e.g. missing dependencies), not routine logging or analytics.

When this policy changes, we update the Last updated date above. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
