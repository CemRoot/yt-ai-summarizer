Privacy Policy

We believe in radical transparency. This extension collects zero personal data, runs no analytics, and keeps everything local to your browser. Your AI keys, your summaries, your control.

Zero telemetry Local-first storage Your keys, your data

Data We Access

  • YouTube transcripts — extracted only when you click "Summarize". We read the publicly available captions from the video page you are viewing.
  • Your API keys (Groq, Ollama, Gemini TTS) — stored locally in your browser's extension storage for authentication only.
  • Extension preferences — provider, model, output language, cache toggles. All stored locally.

How Data Is Used

  • Transcript text is sent to your chosen AI provider (Groq or Ollama Cloud) solely for summarization. The response is returned directly to your browser.
  • Podcast script (AI-generated dialogue) is sent to Google Gemini TTS solely for audio generation. Audio is played and optionally cached locally.
  • API keys are used only for authenticating requests. They are never sent anywhere other than the provider you selected.
  • No personal data — name, email, IP address, browsing history — is ever collected or transmitted.

Storage & Retention

Everything is stored locally in your browser via Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. Nothing is sent to our servers — we don't have any.

Summaries: optional LRU cache (max 20) Transcripts: off by default API keys: obfuscated (XOR + Base64)

Use the trash icon in the panel header or "Clear Cache" in settings to wipe all cached data instantly.

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with these services based on your choice:

  • Groq API (groq.com) — LPU-powered summarization. Privacy Policy
  • Ollama Cloud (ollama.com) — Open model summarization. Terms
  • Google Gemini TTS (ai.google.dev) — Podcast audio generation. AI Terms

We do not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share data with third parties beyond your chosen AI provider
  • Use your data for advertising or profiling
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect telemetry or usage analytics

Permissions

Chrome APIs used:

storage — save keys & preferences locally activeTab — read captions on the current tab

Host permissions:

youtube.com api.groq.com ollama.com generativelanguage.googleapis.com

Your Rights

  • Clear cached summaries and transcripts from the panel at any time.
  • Revoke your Groq key at console.groq.com/keys
  • Revoke your Ollama key at ollama.com/settings/keys
  • Revoke your Gemini key at aistudio.google.com/apikey
  • Remove the extension to delete all stored data permanently.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.
